7. You Can Thrive in Your Ministry Despite Being Shy

(15 min.) Discussion.

Would you describe yourself as someone who is shy? Do you generally prefer to go unnoticed? Do you get a knot in your stomach at the thought of talking to others? At times, being shy can hold us back from doing things that we would like to do. However, many who have struggled with shyness have been able to reach out and even thrive in their ministry. What can we learn from their example?

Play the VIDEO Giving My All Despite Being Shy. Then ask the audience:

  • How has Sister Lee benefited from applying her grandmother’s advice to “put everything into serving Jehovah”?

The Bible indicates that Moses, Jeremiah, and Timothy may have struggled with shyness. (Ex 3:11; 4:10; Jer 1:6-8; 1Ti 4:12) Still, they were able to accomplish great things in Jehovah’s service because he was with them. (Ex 4:12; Jer 20:11; 2Ti 1:6-8)

Read Isaiah 43:1, 2. Then ask the audience:

  • What promise does Jehovah make to his worshippers?

How can Jehovah help those who are shy to thrive in their ministry today?

Play the VIDEO How Your Baptism Leads to More Happiness​—Excerpt. Then ask the audience:

  • How has Sister Jackson felt Jehovah’s power and support in her ministry?

  • How can the ministry help a person who is shy?
