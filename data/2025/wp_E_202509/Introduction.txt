
Introduction



Do you long to live in a world without war or violent conflict? For many, that idea sounds appealing but unrealistic. The Bible reveals why mankind’s efforts to end war have failed. It also explains how you can be sure that worldwide peace is possible and will soon become a reality.

In this magazine, the terms “warʺ and “conflictʺ refer to fighting between armed groups to achieve a political objective. The names of some of the individuals quoted have been changed.
