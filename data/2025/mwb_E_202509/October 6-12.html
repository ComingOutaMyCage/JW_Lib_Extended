8. Are You Using “Truths We Love to Teach”?

(15 min.) Discussion.

Since its release, the brochure Love People​—Make Disciples has been helping us to improve our conversation skills. Appendix A was specifically designed to help us introduce simple Bible truths in our conversations. (Heb 4:12) Have you become familiar with the nine topics found under “Truths We Love to Teach”?

  • How can we introduce a simple Bible truth at an appropriate time in a conversation?​—lmd appendix A

  • Which topics do you find most effective in your territory?

  • What could you do to become more familiar with the scriptures in appendix A?

GOAL:

Try to remember at least one scripture per topic featured in appendix A. Start with those that may be the most appealing in your territory.

The more we use these scriptures in the ministry, the more familiar we become with them. To use them regularly, however, we must first contact people in our territory.
