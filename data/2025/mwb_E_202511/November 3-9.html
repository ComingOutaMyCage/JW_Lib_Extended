7. “The Generous Person Will Be Blessed”

(15 min.) Discussion by an elder.

When we give generously of our time and resources, blessings result. Of course, the appreciative recipient views our gift as a blessing. However, the generous giver is also blessed. (Pr 22:9) Not only does he experience joy from imitating the Creator but he also gains Jehovah’s approval.​—Pr 19:17; Jas 1:17.

Play the VIDEO Generosity Brings Joy. Then ask the audience:

  • How have the individuals in the video experienced joy as a result of the generosity of the global brotherhood?

  • How have they also experienced the joy that comes from giving generously to others?
