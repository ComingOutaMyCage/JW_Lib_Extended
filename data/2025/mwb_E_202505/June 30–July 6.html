7. Encourage Others to Try Our Bible Course

(5 min.) Discussion.

Offering Bible studies is an important part of our ministry. After all, how can we help people to become disciples if we do not study the Bible with them? Then, demonstrate how a Bible study can answer his questions and help him in practical ways.

The “Try Our Bible Course” link on jw.org can help you to offer a Bible study to those you meet.
