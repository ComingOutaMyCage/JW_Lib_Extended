7. Show Respect in Your Marriage

(15 min.) Discussion.

When you marry, you vow before Jehovah to love and respect your mate. Therefore, how you treat your husband or wife affects your relationship with Jehovah.​—Pr 20:25; 1Pe 3:7.

Play the VIDEO For a Happy Marriage: Show Respect. Then ask the audience:

  • Why is it important to show respect to your mate?

  • What changes may we need to make in order to be more respectful?

  • What are some Bible principles that will help us?

  • What are some specific ways to show respect to our mate?

  • What about our mate should we focus on, and why?
