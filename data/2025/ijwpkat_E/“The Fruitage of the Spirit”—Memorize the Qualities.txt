
BECOME JEHOVAH’S FRIEND
“The Fruitage of the Spirit”—Memorize the Qualities


Can you name from memory each quality of the fruitage of the spirit listed at Galatians 5:22, 23?







Parents, read and discuss Galatians 5:22, 23 with your children.

Download and print out the activity.

Let’s learn nine qualities of the fruitage of the spirit as a family! Cut out the flash cards, and distribute them without revealing which cards you have. Sing along with the music, and as each quality is listed, the one who has the corresponding flash card should hold it up and sing it loudly. Afterward, discuss which of the qualities you would like to work on as a family.
