Strategies for Happiness in Uncertain Times—⁠What Does the Bible Say?

In these uncertain times, we may have little control over what happens to us. However, our happiness often depends more on our attitude than on our circumstances. The Bible states that despite the problems they face, “happy people always enjoy life.” (Proverbs 15:15, Good News Translation) What can we do to increase our happiness? Consider how the Bible’s advice can help you.

Cope with anxiety

The Bible says: “Anxiety in a man’s heart weighs it down, but a good word cheers it up.”—Proverbs 12:25.

  • The Bible can help you to deal with undue anxiety. To find out how, read the article “What the Bible Says About Anxiety.”

Battle loneliness

The Bible says: “A true friend shows love at all times and is a brother who is born for times of distress.”—Proverbs 17:17.

  • The Bible contains advice that can help you to battle loneliness by strengthening your friendships. Read the article “Fight Loneliness With Friendship—How the Bible Can Help.”

Cultivate love for God and for neighbor

The Bible says: “You must love Jehovah your God with your whole heart and with your whole soul and with your whole mind. . . . You must love your neighbor as yourself.”a—Matthew 22:37-39.

  • Prayer can help us to grow in our love for God. To learn more, read the article “Does God Hear Our Prayers?”

  • We show love to others by following what has been called the Golden Rule. Find out more in the article “What Is the Golden Rule?”
