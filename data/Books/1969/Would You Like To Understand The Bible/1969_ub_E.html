WHY READ THE BIBLE?

Many people never have. Is that true of you? Nevertheless, haven't you wondered about these things? —

Is God dead, as some have claimed? If not, why does he permit wickedness to continue?

Do the teachings and practices of the churches of Christendom really represent what the Bible contains?

What reasons are there to believe that mankind will ever be set free from sickness and death?

What does the future hold for you and your family?

These are questions that the Bible answers in a most satisfying manner.

BUT SUPPOSE YOU HAVE ALREADY READ THE BIBLE, WHAT THEN?

Well, do you believe that your knowledge of it will enable you to give sound answers to these questions?

Why do we grow old and die?

Where are the dead?

How can we be sure which religion God approves?

Why do so many prayers go unanswered? What must we do to be heard by God?

Are present world conditions a fulfillment of Bible prophecy? What do they mean?

When will God's kingdom come?

How rewarding it would be to get the answers to these questions! You can. How?
