Can You Live Forever in Happiness on Earth?

COULD you live happily on an earth so peaceful and secure that there was never any need of military preparedness to forestall or meet the threat of war? Could you live happily on an earth on which human society was not kept in a constant friction by racial divisions, by international boundaries, jealousies, rivalries and hatreds, by commercial monopolies and selfish competition, or by religious prejudices, crusades, inquisitions, bigotry, intolerance and conflict? An earth on which no great natural disasters occurred, like devastating storms, floods, conflagrations, famines, insect plagues, pestilences and earthquakes? An earth on which you and your fellow creatures enjoyed complete health and permanent youthful beauty and vigor and where never a hospital or graveyard marred the grandeur of a perfectly cultivated land? An earth over which no one acted like the Devil with a pack of malicious demons? An earth filled with the knowledge of a God whom you could love and worship intelligently and who loved you? An earth fully under the government of this God, with his law enforced and justice rendered to all impartially and with righteousness carried on by all inhabitants in the love of God and of their fellow man?

Any normal person ought reasonably to be able to answer Yes! But from our questions please do not think that we are describing an impossible condition for an earth which has been the scene of human woe for about six thousand years. Not at all! Our questions to you are founded on a Book that plainly tells how human woe invaded the earth and how it will be fully removed with the permanent establishment of the conditions we just described. That wonderful Book has the greatest circulation in the world, having passed the mark of three billion copies and in more than 1,118 languages and dialects to date. Do you know that Book? It is the Bible, and its contents and also its history offer us incontrovertible proof that it is Almighty God’s gift of knowledge to groping, suffering mankind. We therefore accept it as authoritative on what it has to say regarding the happy destiny that God the Creator has fixed for obedient people of good will. We must follow what it has to say on how we may enter into that happy destiny. Only by this course can all of us come to a meeting of our minds in common agreement.

You are acquainted with the dire situation all over the earth today. It is more than a challenge to human leaders and governments to do something about it. It is rather a challenge to Almighty God, the Possessor of heaven and earth. The more so is this the case as we see all human efforts to meet the needs of the situation continue to fail, and the lot of the people grows more perilous. The present state of the world merely emphasizes what all human history shows for the past four thousand years, the impossibility of nations filled with the spirit of this world to get along together. The advancement of modem science has not improved matters, but has increased the menace. The great educational systems, yes, even the hundreds of religious systems of Christendom, have not changed the self-seeking spirit of mankind. They have not filled men to overflowing with love that seeks to do good, not harm, to one’s neighbor.

It is self-evident that conditions among mankind must change if you are to escape death and live forever in happiness on earth. That means they must change for the better everywhere on this globe. It must be a world change, and it must occur quite soon if it is to overtake you. Despite the lesson that all human history has taught thus far, the rulers who try to keep in power and favor with the people continue to make political promises. They tell the distressed people that they will create global organizations and create a new world by human hands, a finer world of tomorrow. But such political saviors of mankind ought to know they are deceiving the people with promises which are impossible for man to fulfill. Man’s unbroken string of failures in the past builds up in us no confidence in man’s ability to succeed in the future, even if we have moved into the electronic age.

The only one who can bring about a world change for the better is the Creator, who put man upon this earth. He has not laid upon man the responsibility to bring about the needed world change. He discourages us from depending upon worldly rulers to establish a righteous government with lasting peace and prosperity. He shows us that the way to endless happiness lies in looking to him and depending upon him to make conditions on earth perfectly right for all mankind.

Here are his words to us as contained in the 146th Psalm of the Bible. It is a prophetic psalm meant for us in this day, and we are quoting it according to the American Standard Version of the Bible:

To get rid of rats in a house is it necessary to burn down the whole house? Jesus Christ did not have to destroy the heavens in order to clear them of the Devil and his demons. He simply cast them out and down to the earth. And now why does he have to destroy the earth his footstool in order to destroy all his enemies held here under his feet? If he did so, how would his own prayer that he taught us be answered? He taught us to pray: “Our Father in the heavens, let your name be sanctified. Let your kingdom come. Let your will come to pass, as in heaven, also upon earth.” (Matthew 6:9,10, NW) We now know what God’s will is concerning our literal earth. God stated to Adam and Eve that his will is for this earth to be peopled with a perfect human race in a paradise made earth-wide. And that is God’s will which His kingdom comes into power to bring to pass upon earth. That fulfillment of his original will concerning the earth bars any destruction of God’s good and beautiful handiwork, the earth, man’s native home and eternal habitat.

It was Satan’s antediluvian world that was destroyed in the flood of Noah’s day, whereas the literal heavens and earth remained. It will be Satan’s present world, his organization of demons and of wicked , men, his heavens and earth, that will be utterly wiped out as by fire. That will be the end of this world, but it will leave the literal heavens and earth which God created remaining for his glory and purpose. In the sermon on the mount Jesus quoted from Psalm 37, which says, “The meek shall inherit the land, and shall delight themselves in the abundance of peace.” Besides

telling of the destiny of the meek, this psalm tells what will happen to the wicked at this end of the world, saying: “Evil-doers shall be cut off; but those that wait for Jehovah, they shall inherit the land. For yet a little while, and the wicked shall not be: yea, thou shalt diligently consider his place, and he shall not be. But the wicked shall perish, and the enemies of Jehovah shall be as the fat of lambs: they shall consume; in smoke shall they consume away.” (Matthew 5:5; Psalm 37:9-11,20, Am. Stan. Ver.) Also the Proverbs tell us: “The upright shall dwell in the land, and the perfect shall remain in it. But the wicked shall be cut off from the earth, and the transgressors shall be rooted out of it. The righteous shall never be removed: but the wicked shall not inhabit the earth.” (Proverbs 2:21,22; 10:30) Those divine promises have never been fulfilled in completeness till now. They will all be carried out at this end of the world, leaving a cleansed earth for the meek, righteous and upright people of good will to inhabit forever. And in harmony with this the inspired writer said: “The earth abideth for ever.” —Ecclesiastes 1:4.

THE NEW EARTH

There are many today who love God but who have not been begotten by his spirit to a heavenly inheritance in the Kingdom, but these should not be disturbed by the old religious traditions and misconceptions. Do not think the end of this world will wipe out the material universe and ruin all opportunity for an earthly paradise filled with human happiness. After the apostle Peter tells us of the “day of Jehovah, through which the heavens being on fire will be dissolved and the ele-

CAN YOU LIVE FOREVER IN HAPPINESS ON EARTH? 25 ments being intensely hot will melt", he adds these comforting words: “But there are new heavens and a new earth that we are awaiting according to his promise, and in these righteousness is to dwell." (2 Peter 3:12,13,NW) These words describe a new world created by Almighty God, who says: “Behold, I make all things new." A great multitude of persons of good will who today live on earth have the possibility of surviving the battle of Armageddon into that righteous new world to live happily on earth forever after. Remember that when Noah and his family were carried through the flood which ended the antediluvian world, they did not go into the ark on earth and come out of it in heaven. They came out of the ark on the same earth, only an earth cleansed of the wicked. So Jesus said it would be in the days of his presence in his kingdom at the end of this world.

Certain religious critics have said that this is a “materialistic conception of eternal happiness” which appeals to only the ignorant. But if that is so, then was Peter materialistic when he said, not just, “we look for new heavens,” to make everything spiritual and celestial, but, “we look for new heavens and a new earth”? Was the apostle John materialistic and describing a “silly heaven” when he received the revelation and wrote: “And I saw a new heaven and a new earth. For the first heaven and the first earth was gone, and the sea is now no more”? Why did both Peter and John set before us a new earth, and also the prophet Isaiah give us Jehovah’s promise of the “new earth” as well as the “new heavens”? (Catholic Douay Version of 2 Peter 3:13; Apocalypse 21:1; Isaias 65:17; 66:22) It is because Peter and John had hopes for

themselves of spiritual blessings in God’s heavenly kingdom, but they did not begrudge material paradise blessings for obedient mankind on earth under God’s kingdom. The kingdom of Jesus Christ together with his 144,000 glorified followers will constitute the new heavens of the new world, to replace the wicked present heavens of Satan and his demons who now exercise an invisible control over mankind and bring great woes upon them to drive them against God’s kingdom by Christ The “new earth” will be the new organization of righteousness and peace which God’s kingdom will put in operation among people of good will all over our terrestrial globe.

GAINING ENTRANCE INTO IT

After describing charmingly the peaceful conditions which will obtain in the coming “new earth” in which even a little child will lead animal creatures formerly dangerous to man, Isaiah’s prophecy climaxes it, saying: “They shall not hurt nor destroy in all my holy mountain; for the earth shall be full of the knowledge of Jehovah, as the waters cover the sea.” (Isaiah 11:1-9, Am. Stan. Ver.) Seeing that a new earth is before mankind, the question with many lovers of life and happiness is, How can I gain entrance into that new earth? What must I do now, while the opportunity is open to survive into it without needing to go down into the grave? God’s Word does not leave that question of the hour without answer. You must recognize that Jehovah’s anointed King, Jesus Christ, now rules in the heavens and will soon dash the nations of earth to pieces like potter’s vessels struck with a rod of iron. You must therefore forsake the course of this old world and

CAN YOU LIVE FOREVER IN HAPPINESS ON EARTH? 27 seek his favor and proclaim his kingdom to others. That requirement applies, too, to any political rulers and judges who want to escape and survive, for to them it is written in stern words: “And now, O kings, act wisely, be instructed, O judges of earth, serve ye Jehovah with fear, and rejoice with trembling. Kiss the Chosen One, lest he be angry, and ye lose the way, when His anger bumeth but a little. O the happiness of all trusting in Him!” (Psalm 2:8-12, Young) So get the vision of God’s established kingdom and the approaching new world and keep God’s law in harmony with that vision, for it is written: “Where there is no vision, the people break loose; but those who obey the law—happy are they!”—Proverbs 29:18, An American Translation.

Amid a world of increasing woes you can begin a happiness which will never end, by taking the course God’s Word marks out for your salvation. Like the sheep in Jesus’ parable of the sheep and goats, be considerate to the least of Christ’s brethren who are to reign with him in the Kingdom. Do good to these who are poor in this world and hated and persecuted internationally. For a certainty this course leads to deliverance, for we read: “O the happiness of him who is acting wisely unto the poor, in a day of evil doth Jehovah deliver him. Jehovah doth preserve him and revive him, he is happy in the land, and Thou givest him not into the will of his enemies.” (Psalm 41:1,2, Young) To guide you to preservation the inspired psalmist says further: “Depart from evil, and do good; and dwell for evermore. For Jehovah loveth justice, and forsaketh not his saints; they are preserved for ever: but the seed of the wicked shall be cut off. The righteous shall

inherit the land, and dwell therein for ever. Wait for Jehovah, and keep his way, and he will exalt thee to inherit the land: when the wicked are cut off, thou shalt see it.” (Psalm 37:27-29,34, Am. Stan. Ver.) But you yourself will not be cut off.

The apostle Peter once quoted from Psalm 34, and this is the part from,which he quoted: “Taste ye and see that Jehovah is good, O the happiness of the man who trusteth in Him. . . . Who is the man that is desiring life? Loving days to see good? Keep thy tongue from evil, and thy lips from speaking deceit. Turn aside from evil and do good, seek peace and pursue it. The eyes of Jehovah are unto the righteous, and His ears unto their cry.”—Psalm 34:8-15, Young; 1 Peter 3:10-12.

The whole tenor of the message from God’s Word for this crucial day is that you acquaint yourself with Jehovah God and with his King of the new world, if you want to live forever and be happy. The Son of God, on the night before he died for your eternal happiness, said: “This means everlasting life, their taking in knowledge of you, the only true God, and of the one whom you sent forth, Jesus Christ.” (John 17:3, NW) So study your Bible. In many lands totalitarian governments are making it hard for the people to take in this life-giving knowledge, but let all lovers of life in the new world seize the opportunity to take in the vital knowledge now while they can. In no other way may you hope to be protected and to survive when the day of divine anger breaks upon this world. To you goes the call from Zephaniah 2:2,3: “Before the fierce anger of Jehovah come upon you, before the day of Jehovah’s anger come upon you. Seek ye Jehovah, all ye meek of

Some of you may now be aged and near a natural death. But whether you live long enough to survive the battle of Armageddon or whether you succumb before then to the death which you inherited from Adam, your now taking in knowledge of the only true God and his Christ is necessary for your everlasting life. Then even if you pass out of this life before Armageddon and are not permitted to see the extermination there of all the wicked and the glorious incoming of the new world, the King assures you that you will live again to see the new world with him in undisputed power over the earth. At the time that he raised his beloved friend Lazarus from the grave he said: “I am the resurrection and the life. He that exercises faith in me, even though he dies, will come to life, and everyone that is living and exercises faith in me will never die at all.” (John 11:25,26, NW) What a wealth of hope those words of the King hold out for the dead in the graves!

Yes, but what a stirring hope those same words hold out for those who are hid in the day of Jehovah’s anger and who live through into the world of new heavens and a new earth! “Everyone that is living and exercises faith in me will never die at all.” If you survive into the new world, the King Jesus Christ will make good his own words to you. By avoiding Adam and Eve’s example in Eden and by rendering Jehovah’s King your unswerving faith and obedience, you will never die at all, but live forever in happiness on earth. The

King died as a ransom sacrifice in order to provide that for you. He reigns in his kingdom to give you this precious gift of life. He will give it to you in perpetual youth in the earthly paradise of pleasure which his government will renew on our earth. (Job 33:24,25) Not only that, but the King who is the resurrection and the life will call forth all the dead who are in the graves. He will set before the earthly dead the opportunity of endless happiness in the paradise earth on condition of their unbreakable allegiance to him. He made certain of that when he said to the friendly criminal who asked to be remembered when Jesus got into his kingdom: “Truly I tell you today, You will be with me in Paradise.”—Luke 23:42,43, NW; John' 5:28, 29.

It is here on earth that the death traceable to Adam’s original sin has reigned and filled mankind’s life with sorrow and grief. It is here on earth that Christ’s kingdom of a thousand years will wipe out this monstrous enemy of mankind. The divine promise is positive to that effect, in these words; “For he must rule as king until God has put all enemies under his feet. As the last enemy, death is to be destroyed.” (1 Corinthians 15:25,26, NW) Harmoniously with this, the apostle John, after beholding the establishment of the new earth, said: “God himself will be with them. And he will wipe out every tear from their eyes, and death will be no more, neither will mourning nor outcry nor pain be any more. The former things have passed away.” (Revelation 21:3,4, NW) By the gift of everlasting life to loyal and obedient mankind and by the government which will rest upon his shoulder Jesus Christ the Son of Almighty God will prove to you that he is

“Wonderful, Counsellor, Mighty God, everlasting Father, Prince of Peace”; and “of the increase of his government and of peace there shall be no end”.—Isaiah 9:6,7, Am. Stan. Ver.

Can you live forever in happiness on earth in a righteous new world under such a King? Countless men and women of good will are destined to do so, even from this generation. May you grasp the present opportunity to be one of them.
