AFTER ARMAGEDDON — GOD’S NEW WORLD

ARMAGEDDON will be the worst thing ever to hit the earth within the history of man.

God’s new world will be the best thing ever to come to distressed mankind and will never pass away. For us to know that the best immediately follows the worst gives us courage to consider the subject of our discussion. We cannot have the new world without first Armageddon. So if we have yet to endure Armageddon, it will be better for us to face it with understanding, in the hope of surviving and entering into a new world of God’s making, a world altogether different from the one mankind has known now for thousands of years, to its sorrow. Armageddon will prove a great blessing in disguise. Our proper concern is how we may prepare that we may not be irreparably hurt by it but may endure it and live to enjoy the grand new things of God’s world of righteousness.

At critical times men in public office and prominent in world affairs have spoken of Armageddon as something that can be avoided by the skillful efforts of men. Because of the background of the name “Armageddon” some men have taken it to mean a decisive conflict and they have used the word in a way to fit a certain occasion. In the campaign year of 1912 the Boston Globe quoted the

aforetime American president Theodore Roosevelt as saying: “With unflinching heart and undimmed eye, we stand at Armageddon and we battle for the Lord.” After which the Globe quoted the Bible scripture to which Mr. Roosevelt referred, in Revelation, chapter sixteen, verses sixteen to eighteen. But if battling for the Lord God Almighty meant running for the presidency of the United States, then God Almighty lost the battle, for Mr. Roosevelt lost in the presidential election, and the man, Woodrow Wilson, who wanted to make the world safe for democracy won. No, that hot political campaign was not Armageddon, and the Lord God Almighty did not fight in it or have any man fighting in it for Him. He takes no part in the politics of this old world.

More recently, on September 2,1945, just following Japan’s surrender on the battleship Missouri, General of the American Army Douglas MacArthur said: “Men since the beginning of time have sought peace. Various methods through the ages have been attempted to devise an international process to prevent or settle disputes between nations. . . . Military alliances, balances of power, leagues of nations, all in turn failed, leaving the only path to be by way of the crucible of war. The utter destructiveness of war now blocks out this alternative. We have had our last chance. If we will not devise some greater and more equitable system, our Armageddon will be at our door. The problem basically is theological and involves a spiritual recrudescence, an improvement of human character that will synchronize with our almost matchless advances in science, art, literature and all material and cultural developments of the past 2,000 years. It must be of the spirit if we are to save the flesh.” Six years later, under the shadow of the great Communist menace, General MacArthur saw good to repeat those very words as a quotation before a joint meeting of the United States Congress in Washington, D.C., April 19, 1951. About the same time the noted American Bernard Baruch said the world was shaping up to a fight between communism and individualism and the “first battle of the great Armageddon” was taking place within the United States and the “spiritual Armageddon” was already here.—The Reader’s Digest, March 1951.

The problem, said General MacArthur, was basically theological and involved a spiritual revival; and from the theological quarter have come expressions of fear of Armageddon. At a time when the Fascist dictator Mussolini had some differences with the Vatican, a United Press dispatch, dated February 1, 1934, from Paris, was published throughout the nation. It said: “A plea by the Pope for a world-wide spiritual revival to avoid Armageddon was contained today in a dispatch from the Rome correspondent of the newspaper Intransigeant. The correspondent said the Pope, in an interview, denounced hypernationalism, emphasis on materialism and sex in education, and the military training of youth under the guise of athletics.” (Baltimore Post, February 1, 1934) This papal warning of Armageddon was repeated from the Roman Catholic pulpit later that same month, according to the New York Times, of February 23, 1934, which said: “From the pulpit of St Patrick’s Cathedral, the present ‘sorry mess that has been made of things in the world’ was blamed on Martin Luther and his associates yesterday by Father Joseph C. Fleming, pastor of the Church of St. John the Evangelist, White Plains, N.Y. ‘Whether we know it or not, the battle lines are forming for Armageddon, and the most disheartening reflection at the moment is the world’s indifference to this tremendous fact. The rotting kingdom of the world is fast being divided into two camps—an extreme communism, anti-human and anti-God, and the church of Christ, divinely fashioned to encompass and protect the archives of God’s revelation.’ ”

The foregoing charge that communism was a by-product of the Protestant Reformation was repeated by a Roman Catholic monsignor in the pulpit of the same St. Patrick’s Cathedral on Sunday, June 7, this year, much to the resentment of Protestant clergymen. (New York Times, June 15, 1953) Responsibility for Armageddon is thus being charged by Catholics to Protestants.

Contrary to the usage of the name by politicians, military men and clergymen, Armageddon is no mere stormy conflict between two .political parties within a nation; it is no mere atomic war between blocs of nations for the domination of the earth—the much-feared World War HI; it is not the climax of the fight between the hateful Communist camp and Christendom’s most powerful religious system and its political, commercial and religious allies. Armageddon is what the sacred Scriptures of divine prophecy call it, “the war of the great day of God the Almighty,” the war in which the Creator of heaven and earth will face all his foes and will fight as he did in the days of old. God the Almighty foresaw the necessity for that war in due time and he foretold its coming.

That war is therefore unavoidable. The facts of modem history prove we are in the “day of God the Almighty” and his war is near.

This war or battle takes its name from the symbolic place where it is to be fought, “the place that is called in Hebrew Har-Magedon,” or, “Armageddon,” The name means “Mountain of Megiddo.” The name “Megiddo” is understood to mean the “place of the assembly of troops.” The name “Megiddo” is historic. It named an ancient city on the plain of Esdraelon in Palestine and about fifty-five miles north of Jerusalem. Strongly fortified, Megiddo commanded an important pass from the north into the plain, and nearly all notices of it in the Holy Bible are connected with military transactions. Not that the war of all wars is to be fought out at the literal location of ancient Megiddo, but that the war of that great day of God the Almighty will have consequences like those of ancient battles at Megiddo, for then Almighty God fought to prove his sovereignty over the earth and to rescue his people upon whom his name “Jehovah” was called. Calamitous defeats changing the course of history were inflicted there upon the enemy, whereas miraculous victories were won by Jehovah God for the sake of his name and his chosen people. The name Armageddon or Hill of Megiddo is therefore loaded with mournful meaning for the haters and foes of Almighty God, Jehovah. It denotes the execution of destruction on them by divine power. The question is therefore, not, Who will win at this coming Armageddon? but, Shall we be on the winning side?

The march to Armageddon is on. Our position there in the battle lines will be determined by what side we choose to march with now and by the influences to which we choose to yield now. Exposing the side and the influences that we should avoid, the Christian apostle John, with the day of God Almighty’s war unveiled in vision before his eyes, wrote: “And the sixth [angel] poured out his bowl [of God’s anger] upon the great river Euphrates, and its water was dried up, that the way might be prepared for the kings from the rising of the sun. True worship of the Creator is the all-essential. Man’s confused, false ways of worship are accountable for mankind’s present state of affairs.

Our first parents, Adam and Eve, had Jehovah as their God. As long as this was so they enjoyed blissful life in the perfection of God’s image and likeness in an earthly paradise. It was when they changed their worship in favor of the deceptive “god of this system of things” that they brought an alteration of all things for the human race, their descendants. (Romans 5:12) Their chosen new god did not help them or relieve them. He only made already bad matters worse, and today we are getting near the worst state of affairs of the Devil’s world at the battlefield of Armageddon. After Armageddon the citizens of the world must worship the divine Source of all the brand-new things to be enjoyed. No other god will be permitted, nor will there be any other to worship, for Satan and his demon horde will be abyssed and all human gods will be destroyed.

On the third day after he died the almighty God raised his faithful, self-sacrificing Son out of death and exalted him to a superior position to what he had before in heaven, a position second only to His own.—Philippians 2:5-11.

The “bride” of Christ, who will reign in the heavens with him, will consist of those select few Christians whom God takes out from among all nations and begets as his spiritual children with a heavenly destiny, and who prove their integrity to God’s cause even to the death, the way their heavenly Bridegroom did. So in all its members that will be a world government of integrity proved before all the universe, a lover of righteousness, a hater of iniquity, never subject to governmental corruption. Its earthly subjects in the new world will never have cause for faultfinding, complaining or petitioning the government for a redress of grievances. It will be a perfect government and will enforce God’s law, theocratic law, over all mankind alike.

Because of its spirituality and highly exalted position, that world government will be invisible. Yet it will be very real to all earth’s inhabitants, by the manifestations of its power and guidance over men. Not to speak of what use it will make of the heavenly angels at its disposal, it will have representatives visibly on earth. These will be, not materialized angels, but meh of faith, who proved their integrity during the trials, temptations, hardships and persecution of this devilish world. In effervescent jubilation over the structure of God’s kingdom the psalmist said in prophetic address to the King Jesus Christ: “Instead of your fathers shall be your sons; you shall make them princes throughout the land.” (Psalm 45:16, AT) These are not to be princes in the political sense of today, but are to be devoted servants of God who will be made principal men in a group, or overseers, responsible men over a large or small body of the King’s subjects, and they will be spread throughout the land each with his territory assignment. They will not be bosses, “I am the law here” politicians or political-machine operators having the power of patronage for political favorites. You may look for them all to be tried servants with clean hands who humbled themselves under God’s mighty hand. They will be subject to the royal law of the coming world the same as all other subjects of the King; only they are to be given more responsibility of service because of their fitness, which, in turn, means they will have to account for more. The prophecy of Isaiah 32:1 (AT) will then continue -in force: “Behold! a king will reign in righteousness, and princes will rule with justice.”

One new thing in the coming righteous world will be mankind’s parentage. At present it is popular to mouth the catch phrase, “the Fatherhood of God and the brotherhood of man,” as though the brotherhood of man to man was because all had a common fatherhood of God. The only brotherhood of man that actually exists is because all men are descendants of one human couple, the sinners Adam and Eve, whom God had cast out of his family of children because of their rebellion against his Godship and his law. Those of mankind who cultivate and show the spirit of Satan the Devil have the fatherhood of the Devil. None such will survive the battle of Armageddon.—1 John 3:10-12.

In this world those called “princes” are generally the sons of the reigning king of the land or are the sons of nobility and are a select few, in a class different from the king’s other subjects. In the new world the princes then appointed throughout all the land will be sons of the King Jesus Christ. But they will not be his children more than all others who submit to the Kingdom will be. Why not? Because all the subjects of Jesus Christ will become his children. This will not be so in a figurative sense. It will be so in a very literal way, for all humans alike, the princes and those over whom they have oversight, will receive their life through Jesus Christ. He gave his perfect human life that he might redeem for them the life that our first human father Adam had forfeited for all his descendants by sinning in Eden. (Matthew 20:28) He is the ransomer of all who become his obedient subjects. In this way he will have fulfilled upon him the prophecy: “The government shall be upon his shoulder: and his name shall be called Wonderful, Counsellor, Mighty God, Everlasting Father, Prince of Peace.” (Isaiah 9:6, AS) He being the Everlasting Father, they may be his children everlastingly. His government over them will be a paternal government, giving every subject the loving care, guidance and provision of an affectionate father. Although it will be a government of justice and unchallengeable power, it will be a rulership of love. How much mankind has needed such a rule of love instead of oppression and exploitation by parasite politicians!

The King will see to it that his subjects have a home worthy of a family of his, worthy of children of the King. He will see to it that they have a home the equal of that of the first humans in Eden, a paradise. As he hung upon the torture stake on Calvary to give his human life as a ransom for mankind, did he not say to the sympathetic evildoer who asked to be remembered in Christ’s kingdom: “Truly I say to you today, You will be with me in Paradise”? (Luke 23:42, 43, Lamsa; also Rotherham, Cureton, and NW) Jesus was there prophesying to this dying malefactor that when he came into his kingdom he would restore Paradise to earth, not merely in the former territory of Eden but all over the earth, on all continents and islands. And if that sympathetic malefactor with a spark of faith in Jesus will be in that paradise by his power of resurrecting the dead, then it proves that the King will at that time have the power even to raise the dead the same as he had it when he was a man here on earth. All graveyards will thus be wiped out! Do you marvel at this? Then says the King: “Do not marvel at this, because the hour is coming in which all those in the memorial tombs will hear his voice and come out, those who did good things to a resurrection of life, those who practiced vile things to a resurrection of judgment.” (John 5:28, 29, NW) The vast majority of those who will become his subjects will be those who have returned to earthly life on our paradise planet by his power to call forth and raise the dead.

His apostle Paul solemnly declared before Caesar’s judgment seat: “There is going to be a resurrection of both the righteous and the unrighteous.” (Acts 24:15, NW) The unrighteous will be put on judgment, which means they will have an opportunity to gain everlasting life on earth under the Kingdom. As for those who do not prove worthy of being preserved forever by virtue of their trial life under the Kingdom, the King’s judgment will be one of condemnation to everlasting destruction, the second death from which there is no resurrection. By the King’s use of his power to resurrect his dead subjects we see further how he will be entitled to be called “Everlasting Father” as respects his subjects. His “bride,” his congregation of 144,000 members built on him as the Rock, will serve at his side as a mother to them.—Matthew 16:18.

The King of God’s new world will be unique in that he will be not merely the royal Ruler but also the High Priest of God. It was as such that he offered a perfect human sacrifice on earth, and when he was raised from the dead on the third day to heavenly life, he ascended to God’s presence and there presented the value of his sacrifice in behalf of mankind. (Hebrews 9:24-26) His “bride” of 144,000 members will form an underpriesthood under him; as it is written in.prophecy: “They will be priests of God and of the Christ, and will rule as kings with him for the thousand years.” (Revelation 20:6, NW) The King’s practice then of religion will not then be a mere formality but will be sincere worship of his own heavenly Father and God, Jehovah. As Jehovah’s High Priest he will lead all his earthly subjects in the worship of the one living and true God, and idolatry of no kind will be permitted even secretly. He provided the effective sacrifice for ransoming men from the death inherited from Adam and for their cleansing from inherited sin. He will look after their spiritual health as well as their mental and physical health.

In the ancient theocratic nation of Israel, Jehovah’s high priest and underpriests served as health inspectors and saw that the people had a sanitary way of living and a safe diet; they quarantined those afflicted with contagious diseases and pronounced clean and admissible to normal human society those who had been recovered from infectious maladies. That was a prophetic picture of how Jehovah’s royal High Priest will take care of the health of all his subjects, healing them, correcting all their physical and mental deformities, and gradually lifting all those who submit to his treatment to the human perfection that was lost for them by their forefather Adam. As High Priest, also, Jesus will grant them the forgiveness of the sins they repent of while their imperfections are still upon them. His sacrificial blood will wipe such sins away. Like the Israelite priests of old, too, he will attend to the education of all his subjects in the requirements for everlasting life. Hence he will teach them the truth and absolute righteousness; and jails, correctional institutions, mental asylums and hospitals with all their staffs of attendants will not mar the land’s appearance in silent testimony to a depraved, criminal, deranged and diseased condition of the people. Only when his reign of a thousand years has corrected, uplifted and purified his obedient subjects to the standing of human perfection, free from all taint of sin, will he as High Priest pronounce any of his subjects clean and able to enter into the human society of the eternal world that lies ahead.

In this mighty and miraculous and righteous way will he reconcile mankind to the God for whom he is High Priest. To Jehovah God the psalmist David said: “With thee is the fountain of life.” Jesus Christ also said: “The Father has within him the gift of life.” (Psalm 36:9; John 5:26, Knox; NW) For this reason, at the end of his' millennial reign, the King and High Priest will turn over his family of subjects to the direct judgment of his heavenly Father, inasmuch as He is the original source of all life and the theocratic Sovereign of earth and heaven. The great Supreme Judge will apply a test to determine who are unswervingly devoted to truth, right, love and pure worship for all eternity. Those who, like Adam and Eve in the paradise of Eden, let any selfishness creep in, and commit an overt act of rebellion, will be eternally blotted out in the second death, as if they had never existed. The Devil and his demons will be released for a little while from the abyss of imprisonment in order to be used in this test and will also be annihilated in second death as wicked beyond correction.

Those passing the test in perfect loyalty from the heart will be justified by the Supreme Judge to an endless happy life. Where? In an unfading paradise on earth. (Revelation 20:7-15, NW) Thus the glorious climax will be a world absolutely clean, righteous and God-fearing, in both its new earth and its new heavens. To the omnipotent, allwise, all-righteous and all-loving God is this amazing result to be attributed through Jesus Christ, as it is written: “And God himself will be with them. And he will wipe out every tear from their eyes, and death will be no more, neither will mourning nor outcry nor pain be any more. The former things have passed away.” (Revelation 21:3, 4, NW) Rightly the saved world will adore him evermore.

ACTION NOW BEFORE ARMAGEDDON!

Just a while ago we said that the vast majority of the subjects of the new world’s King will be people restored to life on earth by the resurrection of the dead. Why only the “vast majority,” and not all? Because there is a great crowd of people of good will today living who will survive the world catastrophe of Armageddon and enter the world of new heavens and new earth without going down into the grave or ever afterward having the need to die and cease to exist. That means that Armageddon is so near at hand it will strike the generation now living.

When giving the prophecy that mentions Armageddon Jesus said he would come to this universal war like a thief: “Look! I am coming as a thief. Happy is the one that stays awake.” (Revelation 16:15, NW) Further emphasizing the thieflike beginning of the war of Armageddon Jesus compared it to the Flood and said: “For just as the days of Noah were, so the presence of the Son of man will be. For as people were in those days before the flood, eating and drinking, marrying and giving in marriage, until the day that Noah entered into the ark; and they took no note until the flood came and swept them all away, so the presence of the Son of man will be.” (Matthew 24:36-39, NW) The flood swept them all away except Noah and the seven members of his family and the ark-load of lower animals. This foreshadowed that the war of Armageddon will sweep all mankind away except those who now come under God’s new system of things in subjection to the Greater Noah, Jesus Christ. As there were eight human survivors of the ancient global flood, so through God’s miraculous power there will be human survivors of the war of Armageddon.

The opportunity to live forever on earth under God’s kingdom will be open to the Armageddon survivors of good will. Jesus Christ, the King of the new world, once said: “If anyone observes my word, he will never see death at all.” “I am the resurrection and the life. . . . and everyone that is living and exercises faith in me will never die at all.” (John 8:51; 11:25, 26, NW) After Armageddon this principle stated by Jesus will apply to the earthly survivors of good will. They will be on an open road to everlasting life on a paradise earth. That will be one of the marvelous new things of God’s new world.

We have been considering at length the subject: After Armageddon—God’s New World, but as a follow-up of this consideration the question now confronts us, Before Armageddon—what? That is a question each one of us must settle for himself. May we settle it with the same faith and hope that the apostle Peter expressed when he said: “Awaiting and keeping close in mind the presence of the day of Jehovah, through which the heavens being on fire will be dissolved and the elements being intensely hot will melt. But there are new heavens and a new earth that we are awaiting according to his promise, and in these righteousness is to dwell.” (2 Peter 3:13, NW) Hence he counseled us to action now!

If by faith we are getting ready to move into the new world close at hand, it obliges us to alter our lives now while this old world yet stands. It calls for our abandoning the doomed state of things and basing our hopes upon the new system of things and now bringing ourselves into accord with it. It requires living now according to God’s righteousness, worshiping him amid an apostate Christendom and a demon-controlled heathendom. This means our not following this world’s desperate schemes, plans, arrangements and institutions for perpetuating the Devil’s world. Why should we favor keeping the Devil in power in opposition to the God of the new world? In these remarkable days Jehovah God has formed a New World society under his King Jesus Christ. The society of these witnesses of Jehovah see the need of preparing at once for the terrible finale of the old world and the victorious entry of the new world. So they are now heeding the divine advice to take the action just described. In abandoning this old world you have a refuge that God has thus provided to which to flee for safety. Associate yourself with this New World society. With it enjoy a part in spreading God’s Word .and its life-giving message, praising God’s Kingdom and New World to others and seeking out all his “other sheep” and feeding and guiding them in God’s ways.

Awaiting the new world, therefore, does not mean an idle waiting until the end. It means our organizing in a forward-looking way now for tackling the post-Armageddon opportunities in the new world in a unified, harmonious, co-operative and mutually helpful way, as one people, dedicated to the one God, subject to his one King, and all of us being possessors of the hope of eternal life amid new heavens and a new earth. What an exalted purpose our living, now may have in the brilliant light of Jehovah’s revealed Word! It is a true purpose that will not run afoul, but is one that will realize a sure goal, to the proving that the worshipful Creator of the new world is faithful. His promised world of new heavens and a new earth will be eternal. Prepare now for that eternity of all new things, to live in it and be forever happy. Avoid perishing with this old world in the “war of the great day of God the Almighty.” Get ready now to live after Armageddon in God’s new world!
