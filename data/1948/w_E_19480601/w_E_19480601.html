OPERATIONS OF THE HOLY SPIRIT

IN PREVIOUS. issues of The Watchtower it has been proved at some length by the Bible that the “holy spirit” that moved the Bible writers is not a person. Hence, for the benefit of readers who are still troubled by such Bible expressions concerning the spirit, we take up a consideration of the Scripture texts involved.

2 Not a single text can be produced that says the holy spirit is God, but many Scripture texts do show that the holy spirit is associated with God, issues forth from him, and brings his will to pass.
