
Contentment



Pr 30:8, 9; 1Ti 6:6-8; Heb 13:5
See also Mt 6:11, 19-21, 24-33; Ac 20:35; Php 4:11-13

Relevant Bible account(s):
1Ki 17:8-16​—Jehovah rewards the widow of Zarephath, not with material abundance, but with enough food day by day—​a miracle from which we today may derive a lesson about contentment
