Charitable Works

How does Jehovah set the example as the greatest Giver?

Joh 3:16; Ac 17:25; Ro 6:23; Jas 1:17

See also Ps 145:15, 16; 2Co 9:15

Mt 6:1, 2; 2Co 9:7; 1Pe 4:9

Mt 6:3, 4; Ro 12:8; 2Co 9:7; Heb 13:16
