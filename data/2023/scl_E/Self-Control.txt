
Self-Control


How does Jehovah show self-control?

Jer 18:7, 8; 2Pe 3:9

In what aspects of life do we need self-control?

Pr 16:32; 25:28; 1Co 9:25, 27
See also 2Ti 2:23-25; Tit 1:7, 8

Relevant Bible account(s):
2Sa 16:5-14​—King David exercises self-control when Shimei viciously curses and disrespects him

1Pe 2:21-23​—The apostle Peter describes Jesus’ remarkable self-control in the face of provocation and suffering






What will help us to strengthen our self-control?

Lu 11:9-13; Ga 5:22, 23; Eph 4:23, 24; Col 4:2

Relevant Bible account(s):
Lu 11:5-8​—Jesus emphasizes the need for us to be persistent when asking for help
