Presumptuousness

Pr 11:2; 13:10

See also Pr 21:24

  • Relevant Bible account(s):

    • 2Sa 6:6, 7​—Uzzah is presumptuous and tries to steady Jehovah’s sacred Ark; he is struck down for his irreverent and disobedient act

    • 2Ch 26:16-21​—King Uzziah is punished for his presumptuous spirit and action
