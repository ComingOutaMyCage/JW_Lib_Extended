Pride

Pride; haughtiness

Ps 138:6; Pr 16:5, 18; Jas 4:6, 16

See also Pr 21:4

  • Relevant Bible account(s):

    • Es 5:9-14; 7:9, 10​—Proud Haman feels insulted by Mordecai’s refusal to bow to him, and ultimately, Haman’s pride leads to his execution

    • Eze 28:11-15, 17, 19​—Relying on his riches and strength, the king of Tyre becomes haughty and is later destroyed
