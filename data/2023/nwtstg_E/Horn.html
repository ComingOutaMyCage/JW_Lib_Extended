Horn

Referring to animal horns, which were used as drinking vessels, as vessels for oil, as containers for ink and cosmetics, and as musical or signaling instruments. (1Sa 16:1, 13; 1Ki 1:39; Eze 9:2) “Horn” is often used figuratively for strength, conquest, and victory.​—De 33:17; Mic 4:13; Zec 1:19.
