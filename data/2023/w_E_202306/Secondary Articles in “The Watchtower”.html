Secondary Articles in The Watchtower

Many publishers use JW Library® to prepare for meetings, and they appreciate how it conveniently presents individual Watchtower study articles. But the study edition of The Watchtower often includes secondary articles that contain spiritual food. How can you find these articles and benefit from them, using JW Library?

  • At the end of each Watchtower study article, there is the subheading “Additional Reading.” Under that subheading, tap the link “Other articles in this issue.” Review the table of contents, noting how study articles are clearly identified and numbered. Tap the title of a secondary article that you wish to read.

  • On the Home tab of JW Library in the section “What’s New,” download each new issue of The Watchtower when it appears. Open the downloaded magazine and browse the table of contents to make sure that you benefit from all the articles.
