
Research Tip



Reference Works in Watchtower ONLINE LIBRARY™

Our reference works can help you to learn about many different topics. These online reference works include the Glossary, Insight on the Scriptures, the Research Guide for Jehovah’s Witnesses, and the Watch Tower Publications Index.
The search feature in Watchtower ONLINE LIBRARY can help you to use reference works. As you type in the search box, suggestions appear below the box. The word “Topic” will appear next to any words listed in reference works.
Try it: Start typing the word “Jehovah” in the search box (A). Select the search suggestion “Jehovah” that has the word “Topic” next to it (B). You will see matching results from various reference works.
