TREASURES FROM GOD’S WORD

Continue to Show the Depth of Your Love for Jehovah

[Play the video Introduction to Job.]

Satan claimed that Job loved and served Jehovah for selfish interests (Job 1:8-11; w18.02 6 ¶16-17)

Satan claims that we do not truly love Jehovah (Job 2:4, 5; w19.02 5 ¶10)

Jehovah has given each one of us the opportunity to prove Satan a liar. (Pr 27:11) We can show just how much we love Jehovah by keeping him at the center of our lives during good times and bad.
