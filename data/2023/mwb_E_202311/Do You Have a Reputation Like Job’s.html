TREASURES FROM GOD’S WORD

Do You Have a Reputation Like Job’s?

Job had earned the respect of his neighbors (Job 29:7-11)

Job was known for showing loyal love to those in need (Job 29:12, 13; w02 5/15 22 ¶19; see cover picture)

Job demonstrated righteousness and justice (Job 29:14; it-1 655 ¶10)

A good reputation is precious. (w09 2/1 15 ¶3-4) It is usually built by a pattern of good conduct displayed over a period of time.

ASK YOURSELF, ‘What qualities am I known for?’
