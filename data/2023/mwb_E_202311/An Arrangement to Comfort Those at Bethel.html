LIVING AS CHRISTIANS

An Arrangement to Comfort Those at Bethel

Everyone experiences trials and needs comfort and support. Even those who are spiritually strong or who have outstanding privileges of service can become discouraged. (Job 3:1-3; Ps 34:19) What can we learn from the arrangement to shepherd members of the Bethel family?
