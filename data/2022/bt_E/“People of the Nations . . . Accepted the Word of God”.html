SECTION 3 • ACTS 10:1–12:25

“People of the Nations . . . Accepted the Word of God”

ACTS 11:1

Would the Jewish followers of Jesus be willing to preach the good news to uncircumcised Gentiles? In this section, we will see how Jehovah’s spirit opened hearts and enabled Christians to overcome prejudice, giving great impetus to the work of bearing witness to all the nations.
