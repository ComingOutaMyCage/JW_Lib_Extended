





SECTION 1 • ACTS 1:1–6:7
“You Have Filled Jerusalem With Your Teaching”

ACTS 5:28



From the moment the holy spirit was poured out on them at Pentecost 33 C.E., Jesus’ disciples got busy in the work of bearing witness about God’s Kingdom. In this section, we will consider the exciting record of the birth of the Christian congregation, the intensified witness given in Jerusalem, and the apostles’ bold stand in the face of mounting opposition.
