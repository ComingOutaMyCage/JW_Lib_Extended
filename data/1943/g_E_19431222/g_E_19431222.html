The Rubber Muddle

The rubber muddle was another sample of how the politicians can take a problem that is relatively simple and waste a year doing next to nothing. Rubber is made of styrene liquid and butadiene gas. Fulton Lewis, Jr., who made a study of this subject for The Progressive, saw synthetic rubber being made in Philadelphia.
