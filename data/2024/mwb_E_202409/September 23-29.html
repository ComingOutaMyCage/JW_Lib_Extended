7. Jehovah’s Standards Are Best

(10 min.) Discussion.

Many people view the Bible’s standards on sex and marriage as unreasonable and outdated. Have you proved to yourself that following Jehovah’s standards is always best for you?​—Isa 48:17, 18; Ro 12:2.

  • Why should we not trust the world’s moral standards? (Jer 10:23; 17:9; 2Co 11:13-15; Eph 4:18, 19)

  • Why should we trust Jehovah’s moral standards? (Joh 3:16; Ro 11:33; Tit 1:2)

The Bible teaches that those who ignore God’s moral laws “will not inherit God’s Kingdom.” (1Co 6:9, 10) But is that the only reason to follow God’s standards?
