
JONAH
OUTLINE OF CONTENTS


 1 

Jonah tries to run away from Jehovah (1-3)

Jehovah causes a violent storm (4-6)

Jonah is cause of the trouble (7-13)

Jonah cast into the stormy sea (14-16)

A huge fish swallows Jonah (17)



 2 

Jonah’s prayer from inside the fish (1-9)

Jonah vomited out onto land (10)



 3 

Jonah obeys God and goes to Nineveh (1-4)

Ninevites repent at Jonah’s message (5-9)

God decides not to destroy Nineveh (10)



 4 

Jonah angry and wants to die (1-3)

Jehovah teaches Jonah mercy (4-11)

“Is it right for you to be so angry?” (4)

A bottle-gourd as an object lesson (6-10)
